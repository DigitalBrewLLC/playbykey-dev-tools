The scales module computes note sets for derived scale types — blues scale, pentatonic, harmonic minor, and other patterns beyond the seven diatonic modes. `getScaleNotes(root, scaleType)` is the general-purpose entry point; `getScaleDegrees(scaleType)` is its numeric parallel, returning active degree numbers instead of note names. Variant-specific helpers (`getBluesNotes`, `getHarmonicMinorNotes`, `getPentatonicNotes`) and `notesFromSemitoneOffsets` are available for direct or custom use.
